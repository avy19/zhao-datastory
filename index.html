Avy's Data-Story

Data Collection

For my data collection project, I tracked my use of chatting platforms on the computer. Every time I sent a message on one of three different apps — namely Discord, Skype, and GroupMe — I made a note of it in my Google Sheets.

I not only copy-pasted the content of the message but also recorded the following information:
✑  what platform I'd sent the message on

✑  which group chat I'd sent the message to

✑  how I engaged with the platform (e.g. if I clicked on a link or viewed an image in the chatroom)

✑  whether I was pinged/notified, or if I checked the platform on my own

✑  my mood at the time of sending my message

✑  any non-word text I used

✑  any other tasks I should've been doing besides or instead of responding to a group chat

Although I did not end up using all of the different types of data I gathered about my online chatting activity for the following section, they were all nevertheless informative about how I chat online. In doing this data collection, I learned a little more about myself and digital data in my life.
